Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Streamvik Entertainment Limited ("we", "us", or "our") creates mobile apps, games, tools, and digital services. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use our apps or services.

1. Information Collection and Use

Some of our apps may collect information needed to provide app features, improve user experience, save preferences, or support account-related functionality. The type of information collected depends on the specific app or service being used.

2. Third-Party Services

Some apps may use third-party services such as analytics, cloud storage, crash reporting, or authentication services. These services may collect information used to identify, improve, or secure app functionality.

3. Log Data

When an error occurs, we or third-party services may collect log data such as device type, operating system version, app configuration, time and date of use, and other diagnostic information to help improve reliability.

4. Data Security

We value your trust and aim to use commercially acceptable methods to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Deletion and User Rights

If an app provides account features or stores user-generated data, users may request data deletion by contacting us at support@streamvik.com . When applicable, we will delete associated user data from active systems according to the app's data practices.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact Us

For questions about support or privacy, contact us at support@streamvik.com .
